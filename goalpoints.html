How it works?

How many hours a day?


This takes account for how many hours a day are you watching your favorite livestream. It then takes the amount of hours you watch a livestream and multiplies it by the total amount of points you can gain in an hour (320/640, depending on your subscription status), it then multiplies that amount by 30 (days in a month).

Options


Not a Subscriber - This is the deciding factor of whether you are a sub or not.
Not a Monthly Subscriber - Every month when you sub to a channel you get 500 points.
Not a Monthly Cheer - Every month when you give cheers to a channel you get 350 points.
Not a Follower - One time offer, when you follow a channel you get 300 points (One-time).
