How it works?

All you need to do is mark if you are a subscriber or not, not a subscriber by default, and enter the amount of points you have on your favorite channel.
Then click on the button and it will show how long it took you go gain those points.

The approx. hours DO NOT account for raid, monthly cheers/subscription, Follow (Awarded once), and Daily Streak points because they can not be verified.

In an hour you can gain anywhere between 320 points to 640 points depending on your subscription status.
Every 5 minutes you are given 10 points as a Regular viewer. If you are Subscriber you get 20 points
Every 15 minutes there is a click-to-claim icon that pops-up in the chat-layout. Upon clicking it, Regular viewer gets 50 points and Subscriber gets 100 points.
